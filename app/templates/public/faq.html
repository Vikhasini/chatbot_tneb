Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TNEB email services and helpdesk procedures.

Official Email
How do I get an official TNEB email address?
New employees must submit an Official Email Request via the Request Official Email page. Provide your Employee ID, designation, department, and office location. An administrator will verify your details and create the email address within 2–3 working days.
What is the format of TNEB official email addresses?
Official TNEB emails follow the format: designation_district@tneb.in. For example, an Assistant Engineer in Madurai would be ae_madurai@tneb.in. The exact email is assigned by the IT department during provisioning.
How long does email creation take after approval?
Once an admin approves your email request, provisioning typically takes 1–2 working days. You will be notified by the IT department once the account is active. Urgent cases can be escalated by contacting the helpdesk directly.
Password & Access
I forgot my email password. What should I do?
Visit the Forgot Password page and fill in your Employee ID, registered mobile number, official email address, and reason. An administrator will verify your identity and generate a temporary password within 4 working hours. You must change the temporary password immediately after login.
My account is locked. How do I unlock it?
Account lockouts occur after multiple failed login attempts. Use the Support Chatbot to report an "Account Locked" issue, or raise a support ticket. An admin will unlock your account typically within 2 hours.
How do I change my email password?
Log in to your TNEB email at mail.tneb.in → Settings → Security → Change Password. Your password must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters as per the TNEB password policy effective July 2025.
Support Tickets
How do I raise a support ticket?
Employees can raise tickets via the Employee Dashboard after logging in. Public users can report issues via the Support Chatbot, which automatically creates a ticket. You'll receive a Ticket ID (e.g. TKT1025) to track your request.
How long does it take to resolve a ticket?
Response times: High/Critical priority: 2 hours. Medium: 4 working hours. Low: 1 working day. These are target times; actual resolution depends on issue complexity. You can track real-time status using your Ticket ID.
Can I track my ticket without logging in?
Yes. Visit the Track Ticket page and enter your Ticket ID. You can see the current status, admin response, and a progress timeline — all without logging in.
Email Issues
I'm not receiving emails. What should I check first?
Check: (1) Spam/Junk folder, (2) Storage quota — your mailbox may be full, (3) Email filters that may be redirecting mail. If none of these apply, use the Support Bot to report "Mail Not Received" and a ticket will be raised.
I cannot send emails. What is the issue?
Common causes: (1) Attachment too large (limit: 25MB), (2) Account suspended for policy violation, (3) Outgoing mail server issue. Report via the Support Bot with the error message you see and our team will investigate.
